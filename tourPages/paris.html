Explore Paris

Paris, the "City of Light," is an enchanting destination known for its world-famous landmarks, rich history, and romantic atmosphere. Nestled along the Seine River, the city boasts timeless elegance with iconic attractions like the Eiffel Tower, a symbol of love and engineering marvel, and Notre-Dame Cathedral, showcasing stunning Gothic architecture. Paris is also home to the Louvre Museum, the world's largest art museum, where the Mona Lisa resides among countless masterpieces.
